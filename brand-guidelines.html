Voice & Tone

Our Voice

  • Professional: We are experts in land governance and technology
  • Trustworthy: Transparency and reliability guide our communications
  • Forward-thinking: We embrace innovation and sustainable solutions
  • Collaborative: We work with partners to achieve shared goals
  • Impact-focused: We emphasize measurable outcomes and real-world results

Tone Guidelines

  • Confident but humble: Showcase expertise without arrogance
  • Clear and accessible: Avoid jargon, explain complex concepts simply
  • Respectful: Honor local contexts and cultural sensitivities
  • Solution-oriented: Focus on positive outcomes and possibilities
  • Inspiring: Motivate action toward better land governance

Link Previews

Our link previews ensure consistent brand representation across all social media platforms and messaging apps. These guidelines maintain our professional appearance when content is shared.

Preview Specifications

  • Image Size: 1200×630px (optimal for all platforms)
  • Image Format: PNG or SVG preferred, JPG acceptable
  • Title Length: 50-60 characters maximum
  • Description: 120-160 characters maximum
  • Brand Colors: Use primary blue (#003366) and accent green (#009933)
  • Typography: Inter font family, clean and readable

Platform Optimization

  • Facebook: Open Graph tags with large image format
  • Twitter: Summary card with large image
  • LinkedIn: Optimized for professional sharing
  • WhatsApp/Telegram: Clear, readable previews
  • Email Clients: Simplified preview format
  • Slack/Teams: Compact but informative display

Content Guidelines

  • Titles: Include "Digital Land Solutions" for brand recognition
  • Descriptions: Focus on value proposition and key benefits
  • Images: Always include DLS logo and brand colors
  • Consistency: Maintain visual hierarchy across all previews
  • Updates: Review and update previews quarterly
  • Testing: Validate previews on major platforms before publishing

Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 50% of logo height)
  • Use approved color combinations
  • Scale logo proportionally
  • Use high-resolution files for print
  • Follow minimum size requirements
  • Use white logo on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain brand voice in all communications

✗ Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Use unapproved colors
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
  • Use low-resolution files
  • Add effects, shadows, or gradients to logo
  • Separate globe mark from text without approval
  • Use inconsistent typography
